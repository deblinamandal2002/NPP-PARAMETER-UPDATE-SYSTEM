Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL) is a premier Indian public sector undertaking, wholly owned by the Government of India and operating under the administrative control of the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE).

What We Do: We are responsible for the design, construction, commissioning, and continuous operation of nuclear power reactors across India. Specializing heavily in Pressurized Heavy Water Reactors (PHWRs), NPCIL plays a pivotal role in ensuring the nation's energy security through clean, reliable, and sustainable nuclear generation.

Our commitment to safety and technological innovation strictly aligns with the highest international standards set by the IAEA, ensuring operational excellence across all 24 commercial reactors nationwide.
